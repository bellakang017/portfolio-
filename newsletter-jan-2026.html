What We’ll Explore

In this first session, we’ll explore three design frameworks:

1. Getting Things Done — Capture and organize your commitments.
2. Wheel of Life — Assess balance across life domains.
3. Life Design Pyramid — Align daily actions with long-term vision.

Together, we’ll build a personalized system that turns good intentions into sustainable action.

Whatever this past semester brought, remember we are more than external awards. The habits we form, the people we connect with, and the self-awareness we design — these matter most.
