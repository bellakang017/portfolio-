Why It Mattered

64% North American traffic, 0% North American copy.

Olive Young Global Mall's Dasique pages were direct-translated from Korean. The original copy sells through mood and texture. NA shoppers scan for wear time, coverage, and whether it transfers.

The gap wasn't language. It was how each audience decides to buy.
