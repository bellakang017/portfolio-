What You Need to Know This Month

Summer Internship? Secure Work Authorization First

F-1

Apply for CPT through myIO at least 3 weeks before start date.

J-1

Apply for Academic Training (AT) through myIO at least 3 weeks before start date.

Important: Starting work before receiving your updated I-20/DS-2019 can result in visa status termination.

Tax Season: What to File

1. All nonresidents: Form 8843 (deadline Jun 15) — even with zero income.

2. U.S. income earners: additional forms by Apr 15.

3. Free Sprintax access via ISSS Tax Information page.

Spring Break Travel Reminders

Traveling? Get travel signature on I-20/DS-2019 via myIO.

Re-entering? Carry passport + visa + signed docs.

Staying? Good time for CPT apps or tax filing.

RSVP Here →

Why This Matters

"Do I really belong here?"

Starting in a new country, lab, or program often comes with a quiet question. Dr. Gregory from Stanford calls this belonging uncertainty — the tendency to interpret everyday challenges as signs that we don't fit in. It's common. And it's temporary.

The good news: research shows that when we support others, we strengthen our own sense of belonging.

What We'll Do

Networking Practice

Connect with fellow international scholars over coffee

Workshop: Design Your Belonging at UT

A practical framework to reframe transition challenges

Takeaways

Understand belonging uncertainty. Belonging creates psychological safety — and that unlocks confidence, clarity, and connection.

We hope you'll join us to start the year with community, courage, and extra Fortune — (福) together!
